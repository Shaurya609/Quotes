~~~The Efforts To Fix The Broken Me~~~

Everyday, I drowned myself in the commotion of the mind
Everyday, I hid my wounds so that I would be their kind.
Everyday, I sang songs to tell people that I'm perfectly fine

Everyday, I improved and became a much better actor.
Everyday, my loud cries could be heard even in Jupiter.

They were pushed aside.

That I've been good at maintaining the silence since so long.

And again, I will have nothing to say.


~~~The Ups And Downs Of Life~~~

But what if it all ends? What if they don't love you anymore? What if they never did?
Well I may sound mainstream but you still have to go on with your life.
No I am not here to inspire you or tell you to build your life , find a hobby , work on you , etc.
I'm just asking you to breathe. I'm asking you to stare at the wall with wide open eyes.
I'm asking you to go with the flow. Heal yourself the way you want to be healed, you don't always have to go out and own it.
Dont make efforts.Let life take you where it wants you to be.
Don't get out of that bed, don't stop reading takes of love, don't stop watching romantic movies just because someone left you.
Don't stop crying, oh, please never stop crying.
Cry your lungs out. Listen to their favourite songs and cry. Wear her t-shirt and then cry.
And talk to someone, talk about their beauty of their smile and eyes.
Talk about how much they have hurt you and you still want them. Be vulnerable.
And one day you will get tired of the monotony. You would want life to be in a certain way, you would want to dress up, eat , drink, dance, smile, laugh , meet new people and just be happy.
The pain would last but you will learn to live with it.
Yes, that day might come after days or after years but it will surely, will.
And in that one day life will fix you , I promise.
And I hope that this day I bring to you .

Thank you for staying constant in a world full of change and chaos.
And thank you for just being there when I need you...it's hard to put it into words just how much you mean to me, because words will never be enough.
I know my life wouldn't be the same without you.
We have walked so far, laughed so hard, and cried a sea of tears together...
We have climbed each other's highest peaks, and travelled through the darkest of valleys...
With you by my side the world is a better place.
The sun shines a little bit brighter, my smile grows a little bit wider, and in that moment, my problems are no more.
You are the most beautiful person, inside and out.

You have not only been my rock, my anchor, my tear catching shoulder to cry upon, but you have also taught me many things in life..

Thank you for being my partner-in-crime, my drinking buddy, my road trippin’ companion, my telepathic pal, my personal life coach, my dancing partner and so much more...
