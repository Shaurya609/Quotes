~~~The Escape From Sadness~~~

~~~The Feeling Of Numbness~~~

Sometimes you unable to focus, unable to process,
This is not lack of thoughts, it is too much of them,
Its like trying to differentiate one star from a constellation,
And my thoughts are stars that I can't fathom into a constellation.


~~~The Demons Of Thyself~~~

We stopped looking for monsters under our bed when we realised they are within us.
Often taken lightly and pushed aside for too long, they can take over.
I am not talking about mythical demons I am talking about demons like Anger, Lust, Pain, Thirst for revenge etc.
If they are not quelled you may as well burst like a baloon being overinflated.
For me they are two demons Anger and Lust. Far too long I have been keeping them inside. Far too long.
And now they are rearing to get out and I am afraid one day they will take control.
I don't want to harm anyone or do anything wrong.
So I search for methods to get them out I search myself, I search others.
Till now I have been searching alone, but now I have my best friend.
We both will search together and defeat these demons, I have to be both cooperative and patient.
These demons will resist the efforts made by me and my best friend. But we will overcome.
Some time, some day I will be free of them. And unto this hope I hold.

~~~Forsaken By God Himself~~~

I care a lot about many people, But somehow down the line I end up pushed aside,
Due to one reason or the other, I say I understand and I do,
But it tears me up from inside,
Trying to keep smiling and caring, They say one day you will find that person,
Who cares unconditionally, And many times I have thought I have found the one,
But they have their own problems of who I care for first,
Is that a flaw? Something wrong with me?
In this rat race I wish to see who I care for happy, And in return I want care nothing else,
People share their memories, Listening to them I want to make some of my own,
I go to those who I am close to, But again I tend to get pushed aside for reasons.
Am I that insignificant? That ignorable?
Maybe yes because my care has always landed me in this position.

Its a negation of negatives things between you and the person,

Paddle efficiently and put effort, you can reach the pinnacle.
That's why I respect time.
Becuse time stops for no one rich, famous or well known.

~~~Beauty Of Travelling~~~

Humans want teleportation,
To get from one place to another as fast they can.
But I think travelling is beautiful,
It gives you peace and time to think,
I think the almighty made travel so that we rest a little,
We take time off and be in the flow of the space-time continium itself.
That is why we always say,
"It is not about the destination, but about the journey".


~~~Beauty Of Bonds~~~

They say alone is better,
That you should look out for yourself only,
That bonds hurt and weigh you down,
But I believe that bond with the right peron is a beautiful thing,
They heal, they make you happy,they give you support.
I believe that bonds must be made,
Good ones will help you and bad ones will teach you a lesson,
Thus bonds are necessary part of life,
And those who live without them, haven't lived at all.
In the end I quote Harry Potter,
"Don't pity the dead, pity the living and those who live without love"

~~~Back In The hands of materialistic stuff~~

I look for love and comfort in people,
I put my hope and expectations,
And I give my all to those who I care for,
But I end up being hurt again and again,
People tend to break expectations,
I find myself back again with my books, laptop and music.
These are the things that have been always there for me,
Comforting me when I fall, making me stand again and keep going forward.
Though we shouldn't be materialistic, sometimes materialistic stuff helps.
Thus I thank their presence and care for them as I care for a person.
More than things, they have turned into my best friends and support.

~~~Overthinking~~~

A straight path to a goal,
Overthinking on the other hand is like white water rafting,
So much chaos, so much confusion, paddle wrong and you sink,
The only way to get out is focus on a point and paddle straight.

~~~Scars~~~

I have come to a point in life where I feel it is easier to let go of people rather than trust them,
I have had my heart broker and trust betrayed, people who I held dear, abandon me,
I have had too much of a bad experience with people, I only trust a few,
I have trouble talking to new people, I think twice before trusting them,
Because as they say, wounds heal, scars don't.
